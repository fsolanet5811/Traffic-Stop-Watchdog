Law enforcement personnel work in a highly reactive environment that demands focus and attention to avoid life-threatening situations. Current systems like body-mounted cameras and dashcams often leave much of the scene out of view or out of focus while lacking support for live data communication. Our system fills a current gap in the information recorded during interactions between police officers and citizens during traffic stops or other exchanges within the vicinity of a police cruiser. An onboard camera tracks the law enforcement officer to record only the information vital to the scenario. A battery-operated handheld device provides the officer with an interface for initializing and engaging with the system.
